## Trevor Hartzell, Nevada

Trevor is a native of Reno and handles debt relief matters for Neon Law
clients in Northern Nevada. He prides himself on being a voice for people
who don't have one and has contributed immensely to disability law and
rights in the Nevada education system.

In addition to Neon Law, Trevor manages Trevor
Hartzell Law PLLC, a law firm specializing in criminal defense. He graduated
from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, Boyd School of Law.

## Nicholas Shook, Nevada

Nicholas Shook is a native of Las Vegas and a proud fighter of underdogs
everywhere; in his legal career, he's represented inmates wrongfully
committed, sex workers, privacy advocates, immigrants, and domestic violence
victims. He graduated from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, Boyd School
of Law and received his undergraduate degree from the University of
California, Berkeley.

Nick is currently Of Counsel to the firm, and does not participate in the
day-to-day affairs; he works full-time as a DevOps Engineer for a FinTech
company.
