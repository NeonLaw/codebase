You can use the free service, [Ameelio](https://ameelio.org) to sign up for
an account and write Rickie, or any person incarcerated in America a postcard.

After signing up for an account, you will need Rickie's information. Please
look him up by his Nevada inmate ID, 85902, and the name "Rickie Slaughter."

On the Compose page of Ameelio, your browser should look like this:

<Image src="ameelio-compose.png" alt="Writing a document on Ameelio Compose" aspectRatio={1} />

If it does, then you are indeed sending a letter to Rickie.

Thanks for writing and let us know once you've sent a letter!
